# Adidas-Sales-Analysis-Dashboard

## Project Description :

This project presents an interactive Power BI dashboard analyzing sales data of Adidas across the United States. The dashboard is designed to provide insights into sales performance, product trends, retailer contributions, and regional distribution.

It enables users to explore key business metrics such as total revenue, units sold, operating profit, and sales by product categories. The visualization helps in identifying high-performing regions, top retailers, and seasonal trends, supporting data-driven decision-making.

## Key Features:
- Sales trend analysis over time (monthly/yearly)
- Product category and product-wise performance
- Retailer-wise sales comparison
- Region and state-wise sales distribution
- Revenue, profit, and margin analysis
- Units sold and demand trends
  
## Tools & Technologies:
Power BI (.pbix)
Power Query for data transformation
DAX (Data Analysis Expressions) for KPIs and measures
Data modeling with relationships

## Objective:

The main objective of this project is to:

Analyze sales performance of Adidas products in the US
Identify top-performing products and regions
Evaluate retailer contributions
Discover trends and patterns for business growth

 ## Business Questions:
### Sales Performance
What is the total sales revenue and profit generated?
How do sales trends vary over time (monthly/yearly)?
What is the average selling price per unit?
### Product Insights
Which product categories generate the highest revenue?
What are the top-selling products by units sold?
Which products have high profit margins?
### Retailer Analysis
Which retailers contribute the most to total sales?
How does sales performance vary across different retailers?
Are there any underperforming retail partners?
### Regional Analysis
Which states or regions generate the highest sales?
Are there geographic patterns in product demand?
Which regions show growth opportunities?
### Demand & Operations
How many units are sold across different categories?
Which products have consistent demand throughout the year?
Are there seasonal spikes in sales?
### Business Growth
Is there a steady increase in revenue over time?
Which areas should be targeted for expansion?
What insights can help improve profitability?
